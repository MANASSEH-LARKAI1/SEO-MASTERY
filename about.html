My Musical Journey

Hello, I'm Manasseh Larkai — a passionate musician, composer, and sound explorer based in Accra, Ghana. My journey began at age 8 when I first discovered the piano, and from that moment, music became more than art — it became my voice.

Over the past decade, I’ve composed music that blends ambient, gospel, and Afro-fusion styles, expressing faith, passion, and deep emotion. My compositions have been featured in performances, digital projects, and collaborations worldwide.

I believe music has the divine power to heal, inspire, and connect souls — and every melody I create is a reflection of that belief.
